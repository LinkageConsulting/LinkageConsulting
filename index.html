What is Linkage Consulting?

Linkage Consulting is a pro bono consulting service dedicated to providing expert guidance and support to non-profit organizations and community initiatives. Committed to making a positive impact, Linkage Consulting leverages the skills and knowledge of its volunteer consultants to address challenges faced by non-profits, helping them achieve their missions more effectively.

Mission-driven Expertise

Nonprofit Partnership

We collaborate with nonprofits to enhance their organizational efficiency, effectiveness, and overall impact on the community.

Innovative Approaches

We bring fresh perspectives and innovative approaches to problem-solving, adapting strategies to meet the unique needs of each organization.

Why Linkage Consulting?

The consultants at Linkage Consulting bring diverse expertise from various professional backgrounds, such as management, strategy, marketing, and finance. Through collaborative partnerships with non-profits, Linkage Consulting aims to enhance organizational efficiency, improve sustainability, and drive positive social change.
